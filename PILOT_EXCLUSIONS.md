# PILOT_EXCLUSIONS.md

**Status**: Frozen

Explicit exclusions formally defined and binding.

**Effective Date**: 2026-01-24

## 1. Prohibited Functional Capabilities

- Autonomous AI message sending
- Automated booking creation
- Automated booking modification
- Automated booking cancellation
- Automated payment initiation
- Automated payment confirmation
- Automated refunds
- Automated calendar writes
- Automated instructor assignment
- Automated pricing decisions

## 2. Prohibited AI Behaviors

- AI-initiated outbound communication
- AI decisions without explicit human approval
- AI actions producing irreversible side effects
- AI state mutation outside draft generation
- AI learning from live pilot data
- AI self-adjustment of rules or thresholds
- AI probabilistic overrides of deterministic rules

## 3. Prohibited Automations

- Background jobs with write side effects
- Time-based automated actions
- Event-driven automated executions without human confirmation
- Retry mechanisms that alter system state
- Scheduled tasks affecting users or bookings

## 4. Prohibited Side Effects

- External system writes initiated by AI
- Silent data mutations
- Implicit state transitions
- Non-auditable operations
- Non-reversible actions

## 5. Prohibited Assumptions

- AI intent equals user intent
- AI draft equals approved response
- Message reception implies consent
- Availability implies booking authorization
- Human inactivity implies approval

## 6. Legal and Operational Exclusions

- No contractual commitments generated by AI
- No legally binding statements generated by AI
- No price guarantees generated by AI
- No policy exceptions generated by AI
- No liability-shifting language generated by AI

## 7. Exclusion Enforcement

- Any excluded capability is considered non-existent
- Any attempt to bypass exclusions invalidates pilot conditions
- Violations require immediate pilot suspension
- No temporary exceptions are allowed

## 8. Exclusion Lock

**This exclusion list is exhaustive and binding.**

No excluded item may be enabled without a new governance block.
