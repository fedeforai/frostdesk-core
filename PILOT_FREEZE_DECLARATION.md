# PILOT_FREEZE_DECLARATION.md

**Status**: Frozen

Pilot formally frozen and governed.

**Effective Date**: 2026-01-24

## 1. Declaration

**This document formally declares the FrostDesk pilot as frozen.**

The pilot is governed exclusively by the documents listed in this declaration.

No functionality, behavior, or authority exists outside this defined perimeter.

## 2. Binding Governance Documents

The following documents are binding and collectively constitute the **Pilot Freeze Contract**:

- PILOT_SCOPE_FREEZE.md
- PILOT_EXCLUSIONS.md
- AI_AUTHORITY_BOUNDARY.md
- HUMAN_RESPONSIBILITY_MATRIX.md
- KILL_SWITCH_GOVERNANCE.md
- PILOT_LEGAL_POSTURE.md

**These documents must be interpreted together.**

In case of conflict, the most restrictive rule applies.

## 3. Freeze Conditions

**During the pilot:**

- No new features may be added
- No existing behavior may be extended
- No authority boundaries may be altered
- No automation may be introduced
- No exceptions may be granted

**Any deviation invalidates pilot conditions.**

## 4. Change Control

**Any change to the pilot requires:**

- A new governance block
- Explicit scope definition
- Formal approval
- Updated documentation
- Explicit unfreeze declaration

**No informal or partial changes are permitted.**

## 5. Accountability

**All actions taken during the pilot are:**

- Attributable to a human role
- Logged and auditable
- Subject to governance review

**AI holds no authority, responsibility, or liability.**

## 6. Suspension Clause

**The pilot must be immediately suspended if:**

- Governance rules are violated
- Authority boundaries are breached
- Kill switch behavior is bypassed
- Auditability is compromised

**Suspension remains in effect until formal resolution.**

## 7. Duration

**This freeze remains in effect until explicitly lifted by a new governance declaration.**

There is no automatic expiration.

## 8. Sign-Off

**This declaration is intended to be signable and enforceable.**

By proceeding with the pilot, all stakeholders acknowledge and accept the terms defined herein.
